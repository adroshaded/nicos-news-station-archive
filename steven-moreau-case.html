Community Rallies as Young Boy Goes Missing During Routine Morning

In a startling turn of events, the tight knit community of Kellywood was shaken as Steven Moreau, aged 12, mysteriously disappeared during the morning of February 2nd, 1998. Steven, a bright and enthusiastic soccer player, was preparing to head to his routine soccer practice alongside his mother, Jessica Moreau; however, in a heartbreaking twist, Steven forgot his cleats inside their home and ventured back in, never to be seen again.


Jessica grew increasingly concerned when her son failed to return from their home. Initially she assumed that Steven lost track of time, but Jessica's worry quickly escalated to panic as minutes of searching turned into hours without any sign of her baby boy. Frantically searching, Jessica along with neighbors and concerned citizens scoured the area hoping for any glimpse of Steven's whereabouts.


As the sun set over Kellywood, the search for Steven Moreau continued. There are law enforcements and volunteers who are combing through nearby woods, streets, and neighborhoods. No time is wasted and no stone is left unturned. With each passing hour, the community's determination to find Steven stays strong, and heartfelt prayers and messages of support flooded Jessica in her grief and rallying together in an effort to bring him back home safely. The mysterious circumstances surrounding Steven's disappearance have left the town stunned, clinging to hope and rallying together in an effort to bring him back home safely.
