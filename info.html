who are we?

Back in the late summer of 1967, nico's news station was created by
nicocorp, with an original feel that would keep the American people informed on the latest news. Since then, nico's news station has been a staple in the American news industry, providing the latest news and updates on a variety of topics.


Thanks to the internet, we can now provide our news to a wider audience, and we are proud to continue our tradition of providing the latest news to the American people.
